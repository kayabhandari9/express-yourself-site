Free Group Sessions

Our 4‑week beginner series helps students build confidence, storytelling, and delivery — ending with a supportive mini showcase.

Format

1 hour/week • up to 12 students • positive peer feedback

Curriculum

  1. Owning Your Voice
  2. Storytelling that Connects
  3. Persuasion & Presence
  4. Showcase & Celebration

Who can join?

Grades 6–12. No experience needed. Shy and first‑timers welcome!
