About Kaya

Hi! I’m Kaya Bhandari — a provincial public speaking champion, coach, and first-year student at Western University, studying Health Sciences with an Ivey AEO business pathway. For over six years, public speaking has been the center of my world — not just as a skill, but as my greatest passion and purpose.

I’ve spent more than half a decade competing, mentoring, and coaching others to find their voice. From winning provincial titles to leading through Toastmasters International, I’ve learned that confidence isn’t something you’re born with — it’s something you build, moment by moment, speech by speech.

Speaking taught me courage, resilience, and the art of connecting with people on a human level. Now, through Express Yourself, I’m sharing those lessons with the next generation — so that every young person in London, Ontario, can feel confident walking up to a microphone, standing on stage, or simply raising their hand in class.

My Mission

To empower the next generation of speakers to express themselves with confidence, courage, and authenticity — because when you find your voice, you find your power.

What We Do

At Express Yourself, our programs are designed to help every student step into their full potential as a speaker, leader, and communicator. Whether you’re preparing for a competition, building interview confidence, or simply wanting to express yourself more freely — we meet you where you are and help you grow from there.

  • Competition Prep & Entry: Learn structure, storytelling, and delivery strategies to stand out — and get support with finding and enrolling in contests.
  • Confidence & Presence: Transform nerves into focus with body language, vocal control, and audience connection techniques.
  • Interview & Leadership Coaching: Build clarity, poise, and standout answers for jobs, programs, and leadership roles.
  • Stage Skills: Master pacing, emphasis, and emotional impact so your message lands and lasts.
  • Calm the Nerves: Practical tools for anxiety — breathing, reframing, and pre‑performance routines that work.

Whether you’re speaking in front of a classroom or a crowd, our mission is the same: to help you feel proud, prepared, and powerful every time you speak.
